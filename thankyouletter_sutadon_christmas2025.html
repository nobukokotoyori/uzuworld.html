You Warmed the Hearts of Children!

Thank you so much for your "Pay It Forward" gift. Your kindness reached the children of Mactan.

Over two days, we prepared meals in an outdoor environment without electricity, delivering 200 portions of Suta-don directly to children in slum areas. The light you lit yesterday has become the very first spark of "Echoes of Light."

[March 2025: 10,000 Meals & The Global Launch]
This journey leads us to March 2025, where we will complete our mission of delivering 10,000 meals. To celebrate this milestone, we will host a 10,000-person concert at The Mactan Newtown to officially launch the "Humanitarian Economy" to the world.

This movement dissolves the boundaries between charity and business. Every one of the 10,000 participants will become an essential part of a global force to change the world.

Echoes of Light Global Art & Humanitarian Project

"Echoes of Light" is a global initiative that bridges the gap between art and social impact. By integrating creative expression with the "Humanitarian Economy," we provide sustainable support for children and communities in need. Our mission is to support the evolution of the Humanitarian Economy through the power of art, turning inspiration into tangible change.

「Echoes of Light」は、アートと社会貢献を繋ぐグローバル・プロジェクトです。アートの分野から「人道経済（Humanitarian Economy）」を強力にバックアップし、創造的な表現を通じて世界中の子供たちやコミュニティへ持続可能な支援を届けます。
